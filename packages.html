Sample 10-Day Itinerary

Days 1-3 Culture

Sigiriya & Kandy

Arrival in Colombo. Transfer to Sigiriya. Climb the Lion Rock. Visit Dambulla Cave Temple. Proceed to Kandy, visit the Temple of the Tooth.

Days 4-6 Hills

Nuwara Eliya & Ella

Scenic train ride from Kandy to Ella. Visit Nine Arch Bridge and Little Adam's Peak. Tea plantation tours.

Days 7-10 Coast

Yala & Mirissa

Safari in Yala National Park. Relax on Mirissa Beach. Whale watching (seasonal). Return to Airport.
